BOARD: NodeMCU (ESP8266)

			HARDWARE
     		    ----------------

1. A ESP8266 Wi-Fi chip in middle

2. 9 DIgital pins(D0 - D4) & (D5 - D8) = Gives only high or     low value (Ex: LED,Relay module,LCD module,IR sensor)

3. TX & RX pin used for serial communication (Ex: blutooth Module & GSM Module(This works on serial communication))

4. Power supply pins (3.3 V & Ground pin(GND))

5. Only 1 Analog pin

6. Vin pin (Input power supply (used for connecting a 9 volt    battery)) (Range:     7v to 12v)
